Mario:
Mario, Nintendo's beloved mascot, is synonymous with video game excellence. With his trademark red cap and overalls, Mario has been jumping his way through countless adventures since his debut in "Donkey Kong." Whether he's rescuing Princess Peach from the clutches of Bowser or competing in go-kart races with his friends, Mario exudes charm and charisma. Despite his humble plumber origins, Mario's indomitable spirit and infectious optimism have cemented his status as one of the most iconic characters in gaming, appealing to players of all ages around the globe.
